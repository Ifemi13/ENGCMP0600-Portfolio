1. Proposal

The proposal assignment gets to the heart of rhetoric. Rhetoric is the means of getting what you want through communication—it's nothing short of a tool used to gain power. Aristotle, among others, designed methods for speakers to use to achieve this power. The modern proposal has changed very little from this early beginning. A proposal is meant to persuade your audience to accept your plan, product, message, idea, or service.

2. Safety Document

Safety documents are important technical documents used to educate employees about industry dangers and best practices. Imagine you are working in your prospective field--maybe you are already doing so--identify an issue that is of concern to your field and design a document that will be both appealing and informative for your audience. Imagine this document will be hung on a bulletin board in a break room.

6. Conference Presentation

If you wish to advance in your career, you will need to enter into your field’s discourse by both attending and presenting at conferences. As such we hold a mock conference presentation that allows you to facilitate 8 minute presentations in groups and respond to questions. You should use this opportunity as a way to further professionalize yourself for your future employment. You will give an individual presentation on a topic that relates to your field. Use this assignment to develop expertise in your field or in preparation for an interview or a future conference.
